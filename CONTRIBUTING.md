# Contributing to Situational AI

Situational AI is more than code — it's a movement to give AI operational competence through situational awareness. Contributions go beyond pull requests.

## Ways to Contribute

### 1. Write Situations
The core of Situational AI is the **situation** — a structured encoding of how a business process actually works. You can contribute by:
- Documenting real-world situations from your domain (customer service, sales, operations, HR)
- Writing detection patterns, resolution strategies, and coordination rules
- Sharing edge cases that traditional AI handles poorly

### 2. Share Case Studies
- How would Situational AI apply to your industry?
- What operational decisions could benefit from pre-encoded judgment?
- Write it up and submit it as a blog post or discussion

### 3. Improve the Specification
- Help formalize the Situational AI model (situations, detection, resolution, coordination)
- Propose extensions or refinements to the architecture
- Review and critique existing specifications

### 4. Build Reference Implementations
- Create example integrations showing Situational AI in action
- Build connectors to existing AI frameworks
- Demonstrate the perception gap with working code

### 5. Spread the Word
- Write about Situational AI on your blog or social media
- Present at meetups or conferences
- Share the vision with your team

## How to Submit

- **Ideas & Discussion** — Use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Situational-AI/situational-ai.github.io/discussions)
- **Situation write-ups** — Open a PR with your situation document
- **Blog posts** — Submit as a PR to the `blog/` directory
- **Bug fixes & improvements** — Standard PR workflow

## Code of Conduct

Be respectful. Be constructive. We're building something new together — assume good intent, give clear feedback, and help each other learn.

## Questions?

Start a thread in [Discussions](https://github.com/Situational-AI/situational-ai.github.io/discussions) — we're happy to help you find the right way to contribute.
